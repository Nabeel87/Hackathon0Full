# Plan: Launch Digital Marketing Campaign — AI Employee Product

**Created:** 2026-04-11T18:49:40
**Complexity:** High
**Domain:** Digital Marketing
**Total Estimate:** 6 weeks full campaign execution
**Budget:** PKR 500,000

## Task Description

Launch a digital marketing campaign for the AI Employee product. The campaign covers four channels: social media strategy, email marketing, LinkedIn outreach, and content creation. Budget is PKR 500,000 over 6 weeks with a goal of generating 100 qualified leads.

## Steps

### Step 1: Define ICP and Campaign Goals

Identify the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP): industry, company size, decision-maker titles (e.g. CTO, Operations Manager, HR Director). Define what "qualified lead" means: booked demo call, form submission, or direct reply. Set KPIs per channel.

- **Estimate:** 2–3 hours
- **Depends on:** None
- [ ] Complete

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] ICP definition document (industry, size, titles, pain points)
- [ ] Lead qualification criteria (what counts as 1 qualified lead)
- [ ] Channel-level KPI targets (CPL, CTR, open rate)

---

### Step 2: Create Campaign Assets

Produce all creative and copy assets before launch. Consistency across channels is critical.

- **Estimate:** 8–12 hours (content creation budget: PKR 100,000)
- **Depends on:** Step 1
- [ ] Complete

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] 3x LinkedIn carousel posts (problem → solution → CTA format)
- [ ] 4x social media graphics (1200×628 for ads, 1080×1080 for organic)
- [ ] 1x 60-second product demo video or animated explainer
- [ ] 3x email templates (cold outreach, follow-up, nurture)
- [ ] 2x long-form blog posts (700–1000 words, SEO-optimised)
- [ ] Landing page copy + design brief (hero, features, CTA, social proof)

---

### Step 3: Build and Launch Landing Page

Create a dedicated campaign landing page with a single CTA (book a demo / get a free trial). Integrate UTM tracking and a lead capture form connected to the CRM.

- **Estimate:** 8–16 hours (budget: PKR 50,000)
- **Depends on:** Step 2
- [ ] Complete

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Landing page live at dedicated URL
- [ ] Form connected to CRM / Google Sheet lead tracker
- [ ] UTM parameters active for each channel (utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign)
- [ ] Google Analytics / Fathom event firing on form submission
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design verified

---

### Step 4: Launch LinkedIn Outreach Campaign (Weeks 2–5)

Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator to build a prospect list matching the ICP. Send personalised connection requests and follow-up messages using the AI Employee's LinkedIn Monitor and outreach tools.

- **Estimate:** Ongoing — 2–3 hours/week for 4 weeks (budget: PKR 150,000 incl. ads)
- **Depends on:** Steps 1, 2, 3
- [ ] Complete

**Weekly targets:** 200 connection requests/week → 40% acceptance = 80 connections/week
**Message sequence:**
1. Connection request with personalised note (Day 0)
2. Value message — link to blog post or demo video (Day 3)
3. Soft CTA — "Would a 15-min demo be useful?" (Day 7)
4. Final follow-up (Day 14)

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Prospect list of 800+ ICP contacts built in Sales Navigator
- [ ] Message sequences written and approved
- [ ] LinkedIn posts scheduled (3 carousel + 2 text posts per week)
- [ ] LinkedIn ad campaign live (Sponsored Content targeting ICP)
- [ ] Lead responses tracked in CRM with status (cold / warm / hot)

---

### Step 5: Email Marketing Campaign — Wave 1 (Week 3)

Send cold email sequence to a purchased or organically built list of ICP prospects. Personalise first line using company name or recent event.

- **Estimate:** 4–6 hours setup + ongoing monitoring (budget: PKR 50,000)
- **Depends on:** Steps 1, 2, 3
- [ ] Complete

**Email sequence:**
| Email | Subject Example                             | Send Day |
|-------|---------------------------------------------|----------|
| E1    | "Is your team spending 4+ hours on admin?"  | Day 0    |
| E2    | "How [Company] could save 10 hrs/week"      | Day 3    |
| E3    | "Quick demo — 15 minutes this week?"        | Day 7    |
| E4    | "Last note from us (+ free resource)"       | Day 14   |

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Email list of 500+ ICP contacts uploaded and verified (< 2% bounce rate)
- [ ] All 4 email templates loaded and scheduled in email platform
- [ ] Open rate target: > 25% | Click rate target: > 3%
- [ ] Unsubscribe handling and GDPR compliance confirmed
- [ ] Replies routed to CRM and flagged for follow-up

---

### Step 6: Social Media Organic + Paid Campaign (Weeks 2–5)

Run organic content calendar alongside paid ads on LinkedIn and Meta targeting ICP demographics.

- **Estimate:** 2 hours/week for 4 weeks (budget: PKR 125,000 for paid ads)
- **Depends on:** Steps 2, 3
- [ ] Complete

**Content Calendar (weekly cadence):**
- Monday: LinkedIn carousel post (problem/solution)
- Wednesday: Short-form video or GIF demo clip
- Friday: Testimonial, stat, or "did you know" post

**Paid Ad Strategy:**
- LinkedIn Sponsored Content: target by job title + company size
- Meta (Facebook/Instagram): retargeting website visitors + lookalike audience
- Budget split: PKR 80,000 LinkedIn / PKR 45,000 Meta

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Content calendar published for weeks 2–5
- [ ] Ad campaigns live with audience targeting confirmed
- [ ] Weekly ad spend tracking vs. budget
- [ ] CPL (Cost Per Lead) monitored — target < PKR 5,000/lead

---

### Step 7: Content Marketing — Blog + SEO (Weeks 1–4)

Publish long-form blog posts that target search intent keywords around AI automation, employee productivity, and business operations.

- **Estimate:** 4–6 hours total (included in content budget)
- **Depends on:** Step 2
- [ ] Complete

**Target keywords:**
- "AI employee automation software Pakistan"
- "AI assistant for small business"
- "automate email and task management"

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Blog post 1 published + shared on social (Week 1)
- [ ] Blog post 2 published + shared on social (Week 3)
- [ ] Both posts submitted to Google Search Console for indexing
- [ ] Backlinks: share in 3 relevant LinkedIn groups and 2 communities

---

### Step 8: Risk Assessment and Contingency Plan

Identify top campaign risks and mitigation strategies before entering execution weeks 3–5.

- **Estimate:** 1–2 hours
- **Depends on:** Step 4 (initial results from Week 2)
- [ ] Complete

**Top 3 Risks:**

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low email open rates (< 15%) | Medium | High | A/B test subject lines; switch platform if deliverability poor |
| LinkedIn ad CPL > PKR 8,000 | Medium | Medium | Pause ads, reallocate budget to organic outreach |
| Slow landing page conversion (< 2%) | Low | High | Run CRO test: change headline or CTA within 48 hours |

**Rollback Plan:**
- If Week 2 generates < 5 leads: pause paid ads, double LinkedIn outreach volume
- If budget 50% spent with < 30 leads: escalate to stakeholder for scope review

---

### Step 9: Lead Nurture and Follow-Up Sequences (Weeks 4–5)

Engage all warm leads (opened email, clicked ad, accepted LinkedIn connection) with nurture content to push toward conversion.

- **Estimate:** 2–3 hours setup
- **Depends on:** Steps 4, 5, 6
- [ ] Complete

**Nurture touchpoints:**
- [ ] Automated email drip for leads who clicked but didn't book (3-email sequence)
- [ ] LinkedIn DM follow-up for connections who viewed profile
- [ ] Retargeting ad shown to landing page visitors who didn't convert

---

### Step 10: Weekly Reporting and Optimisation

Track performance weekly against KPIs. Reallocate budget from underperforming channels to high performers.

- **Estimate:** 1 hour/week for 6 weeks
- **Depends on:** Steps 4, 5, 6 (live data)
- [ ] Complete

**Weekly report metrics:**
| Metric              | Target        |
|---------------------|---------------|
| New leads this week | 15–25         |
| Cumulative leads    | On track to 100 |
| Email open rate     | > 25%         |
| LinkedIn CTR        | > 0.5%        |
| Ad spend vs. budget | ± 10%         |
| Landing page CVR    | > 3%          |

---

### Step 11: Conversion Push and Campaign Close (Week 6)

Final push: send "last chance" email to all warm leads, run a limited-time offer if appropriate, and ensure all hot leads have a demo booked.

- **Estimate:** 4–6 hours
- **Depends on:** Step 9
- [ ] Complete

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] "Final call" email sent to all warm leads on Day 36
- [ ] Any remaining ad budget concentrated on best-performing audiences
- [ ] All 100 qualified leads logged in CRM with contact details + status
- [ ] Demo calls booked or pipeline handed to sales team

---

## Success Criteria

**Primary KPIs:**
- [ ] 100 qualified leads generated within 6 weeks
- [ ] Cost per lead (CPL) ≤ PKR 5,000 (total budget / 100 leads)
- [ ] Total spend ≤ PKR 500,000

**Channel KPIs:**
- [ ] LinkedIn: ≥ 40 leads | connection acceptance rate ≥ 35%
- [ ] Email: ≥ 30 leads | open rate ≥ 25% | CTR ≥ 3%
- [ ] Social / Paid Ads: ≥ 20 leads | landing page CVR ≥ 3%
- [ ] Content / SEO: ≥ 10 leads | 2 blog posts published and indexed

**Quality Gates:**
- [ ] All leads have valid contact details in CRM
- [ ] All leads match ICP definition confirmed in Step 1
- [ ] Campaign debrief report completed and shared with stakeholder
